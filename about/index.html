निसर्गस्य सौक्ष्म्य-Nisargasya Sauksmya



We are a team of four people with each person handling different roles. निसर्गस्य सौक्ष्म्य , taken from Sanskrit literally translates as "Subtleties of Nature" and we at निसर्गस्य सौक्ष्म्य love to explore the subtleties and fineness of nature.
